In the below inputs always input one at a time

Input for Win Horizontally:
1 1 2 2 3 3 4
Output - RED HAS WON!

Input for Win Vertically:
1 2 1 2 1 2 1
Output - RED HAS WON!

Input for Win Diagonally (right to leftt):
1 2 2 3 4 3 3 4 4 3 4
Output - RED HAS WON!

Input for Win Diagonally (left to right):
7 6 5 5 4 3 4 4 3 4 3 3
Output - YELLOW HAS WON!

Input for Tie :
6 5 5 6 5 6 6 5 5 6 - 1 (six times filling first column) - 3 (six times filling third column) - 4 - 2(six times filling second column) - 4(five times filling 4th column) - 7(six times filling 7 th column)
Output - The game has tied!

To exit a game - enter -1

To exit the program enter 0
